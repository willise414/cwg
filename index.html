FOR SENIORS 55+ TO LEARN AND PRACTICE WOODWORKING IN THE GREATER MONCTON AREA

Whether you're an experienced woodworker who only needs to use a few tools, or just starting out in woodworking and looking to learn more, all are welcome to become a member of the Codiac Woodworking Guild.

"Everyone I have met has been very friendly and welcoming. I was so impressed, that i convinced my husband and brother to also sign up!"

Bonnie Shaw

It is our aim to promote and provide a welcoming environment for the practice of woodworking. We encourage our members to tackle new projects and to share their knowledge with other members.
